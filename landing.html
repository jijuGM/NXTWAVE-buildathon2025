Absolutely. We are committed to privacy by design. CarbonSense does not require you to sign up or create an account. All calculations happen anonymously, and we do not store any personal identifiable information linked to your estimates. Your session is temporary and your data is forgotten.

Our goal is to provide a reliable and insightful estimate, not a perfect scientific audit. We use reputable public datasets and standard emission factors. The accuracy depends on the information you provide. Think of it as a powerful guide to help you understand your relative impact and identify areas for improvement.

CarbonSense is for everyone! It's for curious individuals who want to learn about their personal impact, for families wanting to live more sustainably, and for event organizers who need a straightforward way to estimate the footprint of their gatherings, from small meetups to large conferences.
